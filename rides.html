FREE SPACE

Picnic and BBQ Zones

There are picnic shelters around the Park, or you can enjoy your picnic on one of the spacious lawns which overlook the rides.

Barbecues are allowed at special places around the Park.

Arboretum

There is a huge and beautiful arboretum in our Park, which holds more than 200 species of plants.

Roads in the arboretum are accessible to all visitors.
